Macos Camera App

MacOS-Camera-App is a MacOS app built with SwiftUI. This app lets you capture photos and videos with all of your camera devices at their all supported resolution. I used AVFoundation framework to do all the camera operations.

Features

  • Capture photos and videos from any of the selected cameras in all supported resolutions
  • Capture video along with audio.
  • Open last captured photo or video directly from the app
  • Shows all internal, external, and virtual cameras
  • Live preview of all cameras
  • Choose between all available resolutions for the selected camera
  • Show captured photos and videos folder in finder
  • Flip camera view along with captured photo and video.
